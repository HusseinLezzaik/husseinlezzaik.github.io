After a couple of months working on synthetic data for improving language model reasoning, I've decided to put my work on hold to pursue a project that I'm particularly passionate about: tourism. While travelling to cities and countries I've never been to over the past few years, I've stumbled over many problems in the tourism pipeline that just feel broken, and could be much better.

XResearch will be focused on three questions as a start that anyone planning to travel is usually interested in: Where should I travel? When should I travel? What should I do?

It might seem that these three questions are very simple, but in fact I think that beneath them is a tremendous global market around the whole travel experience from hotels, restaurants, attractions, exploring cultures, and more.

Will be launching soon.
